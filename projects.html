Translation Nation

Communication is sometimes difficult when there's a language barrier in the way, but it's always good to keep in touch with family and friends by regularly communicating with them. Language barriers shouldn't be a reason for people to stop talking to each other, and conversation should be as easy as just speaking out loud. Translation Nation is a web app for chatting that allows for users to speak or text in whatever language they want to, and be able to talk to someone else who may not speak the same language.

ileaf-ilearn.tech

iLeaf-iLearn teaches basic sustainability through a quiz-style web app. It has a learning incentive scheme; the more questions you answer, the more plants you get to add to your online terrarium! That's right, make a cute and sustainable terrarium that can be shared.
